Pride of the Catch

Our members reel in more than just fish—they capture moments of excitement, achievement, and camaraderie. These snapshots celebrate the skill, dedication, and community spirit that make Whidbey Island fishing adventures unforgettable!

Want to see your catch featured? Share your photos with us and join the celebration of Whidbey Island’s finest anglers!

Whidbey Island's Puget Sound Anglers

We invite you to enjoy all there is of fishing, here in the Great Northwest! Whether you're into Halibut, Salmon, Ling Cod, crabbing or shrimping, we love doing it all. If you're a new-comer or not, we have a place for you to educate or be educated on Areas 8-1, 8-2, 9, or even Canada, and what it entails to fish these great fisheries!
