About Us

What we're about!

We are the Whidbey Island Puget Sound Anglers, committed to preserving fisheries, enhancing habitat, and offering scholarships for aspiring environmental students focused on Salmon Recovery. Though not the first group on Whidbey Island, we strive to make it the best, hosting monthly speakers on opportunities, regulation changes (WDFW), and advocating for more time on the water!

Our Mission

We’re a new chapter of passionate anglers, and we’d love for you to be part of our growing community! Join us at the American Legion every first Wednesday of the month at 6:30 PM. Together, we can make a difference in preserving salmon fishing opportunities. Your involvement is key—without you, there is no us. Let’s work together for a stronger, more connected fishing community!

Our Board Members

  • Tim Hicks – President
    Chairs board and general meetings, represents the club at local events, and leads key initiatives like the annual fundraiser and Kids Fishing Day. Promotes community engagement and sustainable fishing practices.
  • Mike Garcia – Vice President
    Presides over meetings in the President’s absence, coordinates speaker arrangements for general meetings, and supports major events like the fundraiser and Kids Fishing Day. Facilitates member engagement and event planning.
  • Wendell Bunch – Secretary
    Manages club communications, takes meeting notes, and ensures members are informed about upcoming events and regulatory updates. Handles correspondence and maintains membership records.
  • Greg Ringelstetter – Treasurer
    Oversees financial planning, budgeting, and reporting for the club. Manages funds for events like the annual Coho Derby and ensures compliance with tax and financial obligations.
  • Robert Harless – Tournaments/Raffle Coordinator
    Organizes fishing tournaments and raffle events to engage members and the community. Manages raffle ticket sales, prize acquisitions, and financial reporting for these activities.
