About Us

We are the Whidbey Island Puget Sound Anglers! We're dedicated to preserving quality fisheries, enhancing habitat, fishing opportunities and giving out scholarships for aspiring environmental students who emphasis Salmon Recovery.

What we're about!

We are not the first Puget Sound Anglers ever established on Whidbey Island but our goal is to make it the best version! We bring in speakers on a monthly basis to talk about opportunities, talk about regulation changes (WDFW) and fight for more time on the water!

Why we meet & why you should too!

We are a new chapter! Come down to the American Legion the First Wednesday every month 6:30PM and help us create a community! Without you there is no us and limited salmon fishing opportunities.
