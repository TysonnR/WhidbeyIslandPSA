About Us

About

Join us as we work towards promoting sustainable fishing practices and providing opportunities for the community to connect, learn, and grow.

What we're about!

We are the Whidbey Island Puget Sound Anglers, committed to preserving fisheries, enhancing habitat, and offering scholarships for aspiring environmental students focused on Salmon Recovery. Though not the first group on Whidbey Island, we strive to make it the best, hosting monthly speakers on opportunities, regulation changes (WDFW), and advocating for more time on the water!

Our Mission

We’re a new chapter of passionate anglers, and we’d love for you to be part of our growing community! Join us at the American Legion every first Wednesday of the month at 6:30 PM. Together, we can make a difference in preserving salmon fishing opportunities. Your involvement is key—without you, there is no us. Let’s work together for a stronger, more connected fishing community!

Meet Our Board Members

Tim Hicks

President

Duties: Tim chairs board meetings, oversees the club's overall direction, and leads major events such as the annual fundraiser and Kids Fishing Day. He ensures that the club stays active in the community and promotes sustainable fishing practices.

Mike Garcia

Vice President

Duties: Mike presides over meetings in the absence of the President, coordinates guest speakers for club events, and supports the planning and execution of key events like the annual fundraiser and Kids Fishing Day. His role is critical in keeping the club’s activities running smoothly.

Greg Ringelstetter

Treasurer

Duties: Greg is responsible for managing the club’s finances, including budgeting, financial reporting, and ensuring that the club’s funds are used responsibly. He oversees financial aspects of events, such as the Coho Derby, and ensures compliance with tax and financial regulations.

Wendell Bunch

Secretary

Duties: Wendell is responsible for managing club communications, taking meeting notes, and ensuring that members are kept informed about upcoming events and updates. He handles all correspondence and maintains accurate membership records to keep the club organized and connected.

Robert Harless

Tournaments/Raffle Coordinator

Duties: Robert organizes and manages all club tournaments and raffle events, from securing prizes to handling ticket sales. He plays a key role in ensuring that these events are successful, enjoyable, and well-executed for members and participants.
