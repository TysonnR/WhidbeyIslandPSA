Why Become A Member?

Join a community of anglers passionate about Halibut and Salmon fishing while supporting Puget Sound's marine ecosystem. Your membership helps fund conservation efforts, educational programs, and community initiatives. Connect with fellow anglers, share experiences, and access expert insights, our members' forum, and special projects. Together, we can preserve healthy fisheries and protect these waters for future generations.

Benefits of Membership

  • A seat at the table with Puget Sound Anglers State President Ron Garner! As our representative, he liaises with WDFW, Tribal Communities, and Hatchery Programs.
  • Access to expert speakers, including guide services and experienced fishermen and women.
  • Access to exclusive fishing events and tournaments.
  • Opportunities to help shape fishing regulations and advocate for policies that benefit our community.
  • Fighting for more fishing opportunities and sustainable fisheries.
  • Learning from fellow anglers about crabbing, shrimping, Halibut, Ling Cod, bottom fishing, and Salmon techniques and hotspots.
  • Being a part of reopening Black Rockfish opportunities.
  • Access to our members-only forum.
  • And much more!
